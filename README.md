# BlueCast

**"Where Voice Meets Technology"**

BlueCast is an Android app that turns your phone into a live wireless microphone for Bluetooth audio devices.

It is built for simple, real-time voice streaming: choose an available Bluetooth output device, connect it, and start speaking through your phone's microphone.

## What It Does

- scans and shows available Bluetooth audio devices
- connects to a supported device
- captures live microphone input
- streams voice in real time to the connected Bluetooth speaker or headphones
- keeps streaming active in the foreground while the app is running

## Use Case

BlueCast is useful when you want to use your phone like a portable mic and send your voice directly to a Bluetooth audio output device.

## Basic Flow

1. Open the app
2. Refresh and select an available Bluetooth device
3. Connect the device
4. Start streaming
5. Speak into the phone microphone

## Notes

- Best results depend on the connected Bluetooth device and phone audio routing behavior.
- A real Android device is required for proper Bluetooth audio testing.
